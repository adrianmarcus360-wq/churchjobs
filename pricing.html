Common pricing questions

What does 'unlimited' actually mean?

Post as many jobs as you need while your subscription is active. No caps, no per-post fees, no hidden charges. One pastor role, five youth ministry positions, ten admin jobs — all covered.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly subscribers can cancel at any time. Your jobs stay live through the end of your billing period. Annual plans run the full 12 months.

What happens when a job listing expires?

Listings on the unlimited plan stay live as long as your subscription is active. You can close or archive a job at any time. Single 90-day listings expire after 90 days.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract?

No. You sign up, post jobs, and manage everything through your dashboard. No contracts, no setup fees, no required demos.
