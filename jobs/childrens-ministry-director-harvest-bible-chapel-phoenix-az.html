Children's Ministry Director

Harvest Bible Chapel

📍 Phoenix, AZFULL TIME$58,000 – $75,000/yr

Posted 2026-03-28 • Expires 2026-06-28

About This Role

Harvest Bible Chapel is hiring a Children's Ministry Director to lead programming for birth through 5th grade across our three Phoenix-area campuses. This is a high-impact role shaping the next generation's foundation of faith.

Key Responsibilities

- Oversee all children's ministry programming (Sunday, midweek, VBS) - Develop age-appropriate curriculum and teaching materials - Recruit, train, and manage 100+ volunteers across three campuses - Implement and maintain child safety/check-in protocols - Coordinate with campus pastors for multi-site consistency - Manage children's ministry budget ($150K annually)

Qualifications

- 5+ years children's ministry leadership - Multi-site or large church experience strongly preferred - Background in child development or education a plus - Excellent organizational and project management skills - Must pass background check and child safety training

A Bible-centered church with multiple locations across the Phoenix metro.
